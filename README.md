# PairwiseValidation_turbulenceTS
Pairwise Interactions in Simulated and VAR-Synthetic Turbulence Time Series


## Data
Time series of the turbulent channel flow at friction-based Reynolds number of 300 are available in [this link](https://github.com/simFlowDataLab/flow_database/tree/main/turbulenceTimeseries/chan300_1)

The data includes `u`, the time series of the streamwise velocity averaged over the wall-parallel direcions at probes located at the wall-normal coordinates `y`. The sampling times are in `t`.
